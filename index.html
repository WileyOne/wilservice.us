What we do

One place for repair, training, networks, and security. Small business and home, not enterprise. You pay for what you need.

  • Computer repair

    Virus and malware removal, data recovery and backups, hardware upgrades (RAM, SSD, power supply), OS and driver fixes, slow-PC tune-ups, and software troubleshooting so your devices run reliably.

  • Lessons & training

    One-on-one or small-group training on the tools you use every day: Microsoft 365 (Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams), email and calendar, Zoom and video calls, file sharing (OneDrive), and basic security and passwords.

  • Network management

    Enterprise and small-office Wi‑Fi, hotspot configuration, home router setup, firewalls, wired and wireless design, and keeping your network running smoothly.

  • Computer Security

    Malware and virus removal, ransomware prevention, backups and recovery, firewall and access control, patching and updates, and breach prevention for business and home.

  • Accounts & sign-in

    Account setup and recovery, password managers, two-factor (2FA) and sign-in security, and making sure you’re the only one in control of your accounts.

  • Personal security

    Passwords and password managers, phishing and scam awareness, safe browsing and shopping, and a trusted guide for staying safer online.

Why work with us

  • No long-term contract. One-off or ongoing. You’re not locked in. We earn your business each time.
  • Local. Greater Seattle. Same-day or next-day when you need someone there.
  • Plain language. We explain what we’re doing and what it costs. No jargon, no surprises.
  • Built for your size. Small business and home. We’re not an enterprise shop; we fit how you work.
  • Security and privacy. Your data, your accounts, and your devices. We help you keep them protected.
